Add Your Holistic Veterinary Practice

Are you a veterinarian who offers holistic, integrative, or complementary therapies? We'd love to include your practice in our directory to help pet owners find you.

Eligibility Requirements

To be listed in our directory, you must:

  • Be a licensed veterinarian (DVM or VMD)
  • Offer one or more holistic/integrative modalities
  • Practice in the United States
  • Have a physical practice location (telehealth-only practices welcome)

Preferred Certifications

We give priority to practitioners with certifications from recognized organizations:

  • AHVMA (American Holistic Veterinary Medical Association)
  • Chi Institute - TCVM Certification
  • IVAS (International Veterinary Acupuncture Society)
  • AVCA (American Veterinary Chiropractic Association)
  • CIVT (College of Integrative Veterinary Therapies)
  • Academy of Veterinary Homeopathy
  • VBMA (Veterinary Botanical Medicine Association)

Submit Your Practice

Please provide the following information to submit your practice for inclusion:

How to Submit: Please email the following information to submit@example.com:

Required Information

  • Practice name
  • Veterinarian name(s)
  • Complete address (street, city, state, ZIP)
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Website URL
  • Holistic modalities offered (acupuncture, chiropractic, herbal medicine, etc.)
  • Certifications and memberships
  • Species treated (dogs, cats, horses, exotic, etc.)
  • Brief practice description (200-500 words)
  • Whether you offer telehealth consultations

Optional Information

  • Year established
  • Practice philosophy
  • Special areas of focus
  • Insurance accepted

Listing Guidelines

All listings are subject to review. We may:

  • Verify your veterinary license
  • Check certification claims
  • Edit descriptions for clarity and consistency
  • Contact you for additional information

Basic listings are free. In the future, we may offer enhanced "Featured" listings with additional visibility for a fee.

Update Your Listing

Already listed and need to update your information? Email us at updates@example.com with your practice name and the changes you'd like to make.
